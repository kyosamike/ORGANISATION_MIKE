Our story in Hoima

Mike Motor Spares was enstablished in 2003 with a simple yet critical mission: to provide the people and businesses of Hoima and surrounding region with reliable, high-quality vehicle spare parts. We noticed a growing need for a trusted source that didn't compromise on quality or charge exorbitant prices

Starting as a small outlet focusted mainly on Japanese imports, we quickly expanded our invetory based on the community's needs. Today, we stand as a leading supplier, committed to fueling the local transport sector from commercial vehicles to personal cars. We are more than just a shop; we are a partner in keeping the region moving.

our mission

To consistently supply and competitively priced auto spare parts, empowering every motorist to maintain their vehicles with confidence and safety.

Our vision

To be the most trusted and preferred name for motor spares across western Uganda, recognised fo rour commitment to quality, integrity, and exceptional customer service.

Our core values

  • Quality First; We only stock parts we would trust in our own vehicles.
  • Integrity; Honest advice and transeparent pricing every time.
  • Expertise; Providing knowledgeable guidance to find the right fit.
  • Community Focus; Supporting local transport and job creation in Hoima.
